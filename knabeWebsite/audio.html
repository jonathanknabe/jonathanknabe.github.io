Audio Engineering & Production

I'm glad you are interested in having me mix, master or fully produce your songs or album.
That way you can focus on the most fun part - writing and recording your music!


I have several different pricing packages available. It depends on whether you want
me to mix, to master, or to mix AND master your song.
Requests for the musical production depend on my availability.


For mixing and mastering, I charge $400 a song, $1500 for an EP and $3500 for an LP album.
Below are the recognized standards by Spotify for a Single, an EP and an LP:

Single:

  • The release has three or fewer tracks.
  • The release is under 30 minutes in duration.

Extended Play (EP):

  • The release has four to six tracks.
  • The release is under 30 minutes in duration.

Long Play (LP) or Album:

  • The release has seven or more tracks AND/OR
  • The release is over 30 minutes in duration.

Mixing

Mixing audio is an underappreciated art form.
Unlike the performers, a great sound engineer should be unnoticed,
And yet we are essential for the audience to experience the performance!
